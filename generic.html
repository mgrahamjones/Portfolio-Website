About Me

Hello! My name is Graham Jones, and I love working with data to transform it into meaningful insights. I hold a background in Political Science and an MBA with a focus in finance. My love for numbers, patterns, and problem-solving led me to explore personal data projects. I've worked on projects ranging from analyzing the user behavior of a fictional bike-share company in Chicago to understanding the correlation between movie critic scores and box office success, using tools like SQL, Python, and Tableau.

I enjoy diving into data and uncovering trends to examine the key data-driven decisions needed to be made. Whether it's cleaning a dataset, building a dashboard, or performing data analysis, I take pride in producing clear actionable insights that drive results.

Outside of working with data, I'm originally from Oklahoma City, and in my free time, you'll find me exploring new history topics, cheering on my favorite sports teams, or checking out a new restaurant in the city. I'm always eager to connect with others, collaborate on new ideas, and tackle exciting data challenges.
