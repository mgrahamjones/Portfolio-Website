Student Performance Analysis: Exploring Factors Affecting Exam Results

This project explores a dataset focused on student performance and the various factors that influence test results. Using MySQL, I analyzed key variables such as study habits, family and school dynamics, health, and lifestyle choices to uncover patterns and relationships impacting academic success.

The dataset includes detailed information on hours studied, attendance, parental involvement, access to resources, participation in extracurricular activities, a student's previous exam score, and more. By crafting targeted SQL queries, I identified significant trends, such as how parental education level correlates with exam scores or the impact of physical activity and sleep patterns on academic performance.

Key objectives of this analysis:

- Investigate how individual and environmental factors influence exam outcomes.

- Provide actionable insights for educators and parents to enhance student access.

- Highlight the importance of holistic approaches to education and consider health, motivation, and external support systems.

This project demonstrates my ability to clean and analyze complex datasets, derive meaningful insights, and present findings effectively to leverage data-driven methods to address real-world challenges in education.
